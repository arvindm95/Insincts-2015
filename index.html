About

Instincts is back

INSTINCTS, the annual extravaganza hosted by SSN College of Engineering, is one of the largest cultural festivals in South India… Come second week of March, step into SSN for this grand spectacle. In its ninth successful year, this edition of Instincts promises to deliver more, in style. From rockstars to artists, reelists to gamers, it provides the best platform for everyone to showcase their skills and gain exposure to a greater audience.
